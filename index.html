Overview
Makuta Horizon Kompally | Hyderabad

We, the Makuta Developers are delighted to bring you a project that redefines the meaning of Luxury Apartments in Kompally. Our Super-Ultra-Premium gated residential venture, Makuta Horizon, is coming up right by the 100 ft road connecting the very vibrant Kompally to the very happening Dhulapally.

Our Makuta Horizon Residential Tower, when constructed, would be the tallest Residential structure (G+16) in Kompally. It will be a single residential Tower with four palatial corner flats in each floor varying from 2865 sft to 4010 sft. These Flats would not only allow the residents to cosy up in their huge personal spaces, but also allow access to spectacular views of the City.

We intend to lend true meaning to the word Luxury in this age where this word is used so generously! Apart from the grand flat spaces and breath-taking views, our project would have many Ultra Luxury amenities that include (but are not limited to):

  • Infinity Pool on top of the 16th Floor
  • Carefully landscaped Terrace Party spaces, again on top of 16th Floor
  • A 10,000 sft Wi-Fi Enabled Clubhouse inclusive of Gym, Table Tennis etc.
  • Business Lounge, Conference Area and Spacious Drop-off Plaza
  • Car-Wash Station & Electrical Car Charging stations
  • VRV AC System Provision
  • Hydro Pneumatic Water Supply
  • Metered Water and Piped Gas
  • Fire Sensors and Water Sprinklers in each Flat
  • Grand Open spaces including Multi-Purpose Court, Jogging Track, Children’s Play area etc.
  • 75% Open Spaces
  • Right adjacent to the upcoming Tallest Commercial Tower (Makuta Prime) of Kompally
